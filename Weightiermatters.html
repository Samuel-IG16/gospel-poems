Weightier Matters

It concerns the greatest treasure Not to be bargained for moments pleasure Which in tripartite man is not measured? You have a soul to save There is a hell to escape A terrible place of pain. There is a heaven to gain Where you do not need money Now is the good time to start the journey To a prepared place for prepared people Though, trouble and in life disabled The shore of paradise conveys security Salvation and righteousness attributes of spiritual maturity, Are stepping stones to God. There is a way, broad and smooth The other, narrow and straight One leads to life and glory The other to death and gloom Brace not yourself to stand the blows of divine truth It may smart and sting, but it heals and recovers Don't take the broad way that seems right, For sin has blinded your eyes, warp your judgment It is a religious road that leads to hell Take the Calvary road that leads to life It ostratizes and buries you with worthy few There are many ways to hell, but one way to God There are many books of men but only one word of God Man has many remedies for sin, God has one the blood of Jesus Religious works abound, only one work saves Christ is the way and not a plan or creed People may embrace his person and deeds Doctrines makes one to be as dead as a door post But practice of the gospel Is like a living fish fearing neither tide nor current In living a life devoid of sin, in a world of sin Like a saltless fish, in a salty sea.

— By Igbinijesu John

Bible references: Isaiah 1:18.

Sin

Formed and fashioned by satan It has many a man and woman Entangled under his throes Hear now or all you lads That is feeling so bad It is a cancer of the soul A nagging thorn in the flesh And nibbling constant cares The right path is narrow and even Its path is broad of evil Arthful in a cunny way Subtle in a rational way Many a life are bristled with woes Consequence on sinful nature Which is attended with ineveitable hour The toll that sins takes in man's life Surpasses every twinkling of eyes Brainwashingly, to you it says. Just eat and drinkn and merry be Forget tomorrow life is carefree Stay up late in revel tonight And make yourself to girls a sight Lo! I say, you will realize with a start Even as your body and soul are apart And being justly sentenced to hell Choose now the good way known And it will take you to heaven your home Salvation is a treasure never let it go Sin is no pleasure Though satan tells you so Though many had tried it Lo! Don't try its bitter taste For too late they find it living their life a waste Sin in revel has many a purse drained Having clouded man's thinking. Behold much taint their breath Insanely with wages of sin-death Satan makes sin the bait of his trap Throwing it to the mind, a swift dart Blend attractive to catch the eyes Caught are slaves they know not why To be saved from it you hear them cry Now come to Christ and get excused For having had your life abused

— By Igbinijesu John

Summary

Bible references: Romans 3:23.

Man

Respectfully and wonderfully created With equitable triune nature Made in the image of God But spoilt by the treachery of evil Man, Whom God breath the breath of life But satan the breath of hell. The spiritual reality in him Is poised between two worlds The daughter of man beguile by mortal foe Roves the street to fill their purse And are condemn to groan. Come back to Him whose love is unfathomable And be vouch safe out of His mercy Before it comes, the inevitable hour His blood bought love liberate the filthy. Like king Solomon I assert mine Go to the ants thou o man Consider it's ways and be thou wise In this will you overcome the mortal foe Who has got many under his throes

— By Igbinijesu John

Who Shall Deliver Us

Born sinner by nature and by practice Encumbered and clothed in its vice We are living in trouble times Life is a puzzle to come by The pieces just fall in places Having to trade our soul for moment riches We have found ourselves in spiritual Kindergarten where bewild infants Are spelling God with the wrong blocks Lo! We can't make head or tail of life Is there light in his shroulded earth Of uncertainty chaos and fertility We have fallen to God penal offence By sin the root cause of our fraity Ah we are undone to whom shall we have recourse Glory! The light of the lord is dawn on us Jesus is the way the truth All our sins to him we spread to get absolution by his blood shed Fret not my soul So long God with us The storm shall rage And never can we be swayed In Christ we are too enthusiastic To be chilled and daunted by setback Come what may in this transistory word We shall stand through thick and thin in Christ From the stormy blast and raging tempest, He guides us through the maze of life Victoriously against the mortal foes Put this in mind also

— By Igbinijesu John

Inevitable Evil

Man lives in tears and persecutions Persecution vis-à-vis temptation Is the Christians spiritual examination? And victoriously their spiritual promotion Every now and then it comes to us a test of faith For lo! It is half the battle to overcome in life With surprise, siege, strategy and subtility The antics and tactics of satan But I know that God is with me The storm of life shall spent itself And I will remain anchored in Christ So long as my vision of him is not chilled and daunted Come what may in this transistory world Over the insidious attacks of satan I shall stand through thick and thin in Christ

— By Igbinijesu John

...Men Once To Die...

This is the prophecy to those to be born This is the biography of those who has been born This is a divine decree: "…men once to die…" This bible decree is confirmed by bible chronology "…and he died." Reccorded monotonously and morbidly. Methuselah's epitaph as recorded in the bible Reads the same as his shorter lived brethren-"and he died" All of adams race died They died of an age in all ages Death is the greatest day to Christians And a time of eternal torment for sinners You may be avowed atheist A blantant blasphemer A calloused and cocky Christ rejector A deceived deluded denier of the "the victor" After death you will go to hell, being rejected Are you looking where you are going? Are you going where you are looking? Are you looking and going to heaven? The yonder celestial haven! Know that where you are looking! Determines where you are going!

— By Igbinijesu John

Summary

A physician defined life in one sentence: "tender teens, teachable twenties, tireless thirties, fiery forties, failing fifties, slipping sixties, sliding seventies, ailing eighties — shortened breath, death, sod, then God's judgement."

Living In Sin

It is going along nibbling on constant cares The lfe is irritating more often than pleasing Timorous more often often than calm It is being disturb, pertube and frankly frustrated The sinner devoid of their life vaguest impression Swim and wallow in a sea of sin This they do without the faintest idea Sinners pride on vague control of their life But you can bet this They will reap their wages – death As satan the goblin will halt their breath Sin bodg's man down in a morals of thraldom The sinners problem boils down to sin And there are several spot of human nature Iniquity of the heart, a sin of bastard variety Brings a life of muddling living. Transgressor the law breaker Abomination a taboo commited Satan makes offers purporting sin's pleasure The nostrums and quackery's of evil measure That stinks and swamps the sinners Living in sin is the bread and butter of satan Serving to cement the chronicity of hell-to-earth No limit to the variety and nuisance of it's art It wipes the sinner out of house and savings With business venture going to the wall Living in sin makes satan camp on your trail Bringing confounded nuisance to you in trivail As you surmise its tactic pians In your life might and main Living in sin is a life Tensed, discouraged forlorn and futile It's laudable benefit is artificial jolly Dithered and devoid of anything holy Sin nips life in the bud before it blooms Bringing a life devoid of sparkle and luster Give your life to Christ and be free from the mortal foes Let this in mind also

— By Igbinijesu John

Liquor

Jesus turned water to 'oinos' – sweet grape juice It is different from shekar' – strong drinks This has been a bone of contention A hive and pedestal for elevated discourse Lo! For ages liquor is the poison taken by men That steal away their brains and brawns They drink themselves tipsy and run amok An unmitigated evil difficult to do away. Drunkenness clouds the intellect and fogs the brain Making them worst than useless drags in society Listen friends! "it is more destructive than wars It is the devil in solution Ah! It is distilled damination Many a man has err due to false vision Its luck is proverbial for it fickleness A cancer also in human society On the quick sand of morbid superiority It anchors the inebriates Alas eating out their vitals The most tacit method of assassination Ever evented by any age or nation It has killed lots than all wars of nations It is the raging mocker that deceives Those that imbibe it invite and welcome tragedy For it brings sin and debauchery Making drunkards acquiesce to vices tacitly For it is their shortlived happiness. Come to Christ, who gives pepetual happiness And above all eternal life hereafter.

— By Igbinijesu John

Summary

Bible references: Ephesians 5:18.

Man's Saviour

Sitting and looking at monument Obviously in a pensive mien It came unfoldingly – man! God has made you king of circumstances and nature Had the mortal foe given you its nature What have you in heart and mind? To do God's will is all you mind What in the heart had taken root? Whither thou goest the good or bad route? A sinner in sin surely needs the savior Who can save from sin grievous sour Christ who from above came the savior Bounds indissoluble are by his sundered Though fortune is inconstant and tempers are soured No tyranny of circumstance or mortal foe Can dissuade you to sin throes Put it in mind also In Jesus's love we can make heaven Even at the ineveitable hour As our wane life wan off To meet him across the azure.

— By Igbinijesu John

Hell

Philosophers may prate there is no hell 'Foolosophy' has warped their knowledge well This they uphold to make a name But the rich man says '…i am tormented in this flame'. The possibility and credibility Could be prove by scientific study By laws of logic , and by rules of reason The rich man in hell as Bible says is for all a lesson A man in eternity a man with a tale to tell At this moment he is in pain in hell Not "just a grave" as the Jehovah's witnesses alibi Not "annihilation of souls" as the 7th day Adventist's lullaby Hell is a real, literal and actual place Listen there is a Heaven to win and a Hell to shun For in hell the torture is more than being put inside the sun A place of unparalleled suffering and torment As the rich man still testify to this moment A place of an unforgettable past In which you listen to the play back of your own memory A place of unanswered prayers, unsatisfied desires The Belshazzars will not have their wine The Ahabs will not have their Naboths vineyards The Felix will not have their Drusillas The Herods will not have their sensuous dances The Judas will not have their cancered gold They cry in bitter,blitthing voices One song , one note ,one cadence, one refrain Forever, forever forever forever With their burning faces, glazed eyes and tortured hands Despair, despondency, disaster, death, yea doom I have penned this pungent poem To you and to them.

— By Igbinijesu John

Alcohol

At first he drank happily Then he drank heavily and habitually First he drank delicately Then drank daringly First he drank proudly Then he drank ploddingly First he was care free about it but soon he was crushed by it A dishelled, depraved derelict drunkard. Alcohol mightily merciless and murderous Bringing broken families and abused children With a condemning conscience and dissipating dearth. A lamentable alcoholic life and sin sated soul Makes you a hardened sinner, God says In the bible …all have sinned. Are you a drunkard lying in your vomit And in your stupor in the gutter An alcoholic who shuffles up the street Asking for death dealing alcohol, making the mind to stray With pernicious practice of lust and licentiousness The word of God brings conviction That JESUS died for your salvation From sin sated sin shackled and sin sickened soul But heard in every wail of woe And behind every sin and sorrow The gun, the knife, the deadly deed, The satanic scheme and unkind cutting words And the whole world godless greed Is what the alcoholic breed. Though their sin is more than A woeful word, a damnable force Through Jesus, alcohol is a conquerable foe

— By Igbinijesu John

Summary

Bible references: Ephesians 5:18.
